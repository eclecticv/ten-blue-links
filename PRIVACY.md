# Privacy Policy — Ten Blue Links

_Last updated: 2026-05-14_

**Ten Blue Links does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data.**

The extension runs entirely in your browser. It has no background script, makes no network requests, contains no analytics, and has no account system. There is nothing to opt out of because there is nothing to opt in to.

## What the extension accesses

The only resource the extension accesses is the Google Search page you are currently viewing. The extension modifies that page locally in your browser to hide non-result content — AI Overviews, sponsored listings, the knowledge panel, People Also Ask, discussions, videos, products, and local pack widgets. The modified DOM never leaves your machine.

## What the extension stores

Nothing. The extension does not use `chrome.storage`, cookies, IndexedDB, localStorage, or any other persistence mechanism.

## What the extension transmits

Nothing. The extension makes no `fetch`, `XMLHttpRequest`, or `WebSocket` connections. It does not send your queries, click history, or any other information anywhere.

## Permissions

The extension declares only the host permission required for its content script to run on `www.google.com/search*`. It does not request `storage`, `tabs`, `webRequest`, or any other permission that would let it access data beyond the page you are viewing.

## Third-party services

None. The extension does not integrate with any third-party service, SDK, or analytics provider.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the date at the top will be updated and the change will be summarized in the extension's version history on the Chrome Web Store.
